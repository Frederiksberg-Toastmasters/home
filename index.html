About the Conference

Division G Conference

The conference gathers the winners of the Toastmasters areas in Division G (Denmark & Malmö Area) in the categories of Prepared Speech, Table Topics, and Evaluation, as well as one keynote, two workshops, and lots of fun!

Where

Kulturstationen Vanløse, Jernbane Allé 38, 2720 Copenhagen, Denmark

When

Saturday, May 2nd, 2020
from 10:00 to 18:00

Venue

Conference venue location information and gallery

Kulturstationen Vanløse, Copenhagen, Denmark

The venue is 100 meters from the Vanløse S-train station.

Directions

Here are the two ways to get here

From the Library

Enter the library, walk through it, pass the bridge, and find the conference room in the orange hallway.

From the S-Train Station

Enter the tower, climb the staircase one floor, and find the conference room in the orange hallway.
