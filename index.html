Empower Your Message!

Deliberate and repeated public speaking can lead to clear and impactful messages and help people from all walks of life achieve effective and performant communication in the most situations of daily life.

The contest empowers all participants and gathers the winners of the Toastmasters Areas in Division G (Denmark & Skåne Area) in the categories of International Speech Contest, Table Topics, and Evaluation, as well as a keynote and lots of fun!

Where

Online on Zoom. We are sending meeting details by email to all registered participants. Register by Thursday April 30 end of day (Copenhagen time) to receive the reminder (and last) email on Friday!

When

Saturday, May 2nd, 2020
from 11:00 to 16:30

Keynote

Meet our Distinguished Keynote Speaker!

Speaking with Impact in Challenging Times

Aditya Maheswaran, DTM

1st runner up, 1st Indian representing India, and 3-time representative at the World Championship of Public Speaking. Distinguished Toastmaster since 2010

Aditya is a sought-after keynote speaker and thought leader in leading in the VUCA world, purpose-driven leadership, executive presence, mindfulness, and personal excellence. He is known to combine the eternal wisdom of the human thought with modern research on leadership, giving him a unique advantage of engaging an audience.

He was placed World #2 at the World Championship of Public Speaking 2015 held at Las Vegas from over 35.000 public speakers around the World. He is the 1st Indian representing India to have entered the finals of the championship. He is a 3-time representative at the World Championship of Public Speaking.

Aditya is a 2-time TEDx speaker and was bestowed the Distinguished Toastmaster (DTM) in 2010 by Toastmasters International. He is a regular columnist with "The Hindu" daily and several other publications. He has addressed audiences in over 15 countries and 25 cities across continents.

Aditya is an Engineer by qualification and holds a Post Graduate Degree in Management (MBA) from the Indian School of Business (ISB), specializing in Strategy and Marketing. He is passionate about history, leadership, Advaita Vedanta, and physics.

Frequently Asked Questions

For anything else, contact us!

  • How will I know where to join online?

    Closer to the contest date, we will update social media and send e-mails to each registered attendee with instructions for accessing the contest online. Register by Thursday end of day in Copenhagen time!

  • Will I receive a refund if I paid for an 🦆 Early Bird or a 🦢 Regular ticket?

    Absolutely! Your payment will be reimbursed in full, and your ticket will convert to a guaranteed 🕊 Free Bird instead! Do not hesitate to contact us if you encounter issues with the transfers etc.

  • As a Contestant, what equipment do I need to compete?
